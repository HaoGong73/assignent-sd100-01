SUPPER TURKEY

Cooking times are for planning purposes only – always use a digital meat thermometer to determine doneness.

Ingredients
Quantity Ingredient Instructions
4 medium Canadian turkey thighs
Salt and pepper to taste
1 tbsp olive oil
3 red, yellow or orange peppers sliced
1 large onion thinly sliced
½ tsp ground basil
½ tsp ground rosemary
3 bay leaves
1 cup turkey broth
½ cup white wine
2 tbsp chopped parsley

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Heat olive oil over medium heat in an oven-proof skillet or frying pan.
  3. Brown turkey thighs on both sides until skin is crispy, 5 – 8 minutes per side.
  4. Remove turkey from skillet and set aside.
  5. Turn heat up to high.
  6. Sauté onions and peppers over high heat for 1-2 minutes until lightly browned in spots.
  7. Remove from heat.
  8. Return turkey thighs to the pan.
  9. Add spices, bay leaves, turkey broth and wine.
  10. Bake for 35 – 45 minutes, until turkey is cooked through and an instant read thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the meat reads 165°F (74°C).
  11. Top with fresh parsley and serve.
