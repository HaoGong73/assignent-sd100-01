BREAKFAST SANDWISH

Cooking times are for planning purposes only – always use a digital meat thermometer to determine doneness.

Ingredients
Quantity Ingredient Instructions
1 (3-4 lb) poached turkey breast sliced
4-6 ciabatta buns
¾ cup roasted red peppers sliced
¾ cup caramelized onions
4-6 slices provolone cheese
3 tbsp grainy mustard
1 cup arugula

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat oven to broil. Melt cheese on bottom half of the ciabatta buns.
  2. Spread mustard on the top half of the buns.
  3. Assemble sandwiches: generously layer onions, peppers, arugula and turkey to each cheesy bun. Finish with the top bun.
  4. Serve!
